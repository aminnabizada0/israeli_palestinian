A cleaned and processed dataset of fatalities in the Israeli-Palestinian conflict. Includes victim demographics,
location,
participation in hostilities,
injuries,
and weapons used. Ideal for trend,
demographic, and geospatial analysis.


Features
Date, Age, Gender, Citizenship – Victim details

Location (District/Region) – For mapping fatalities

Participation in Hostilities – Conflict involvement

Injuries & Weapons Used – Circumstances of death
